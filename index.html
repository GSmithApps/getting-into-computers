Terminal

  • A single "." means current folder
    cd .
  • ".." means up one folder
    cd ..
  • "~" means the home folder
    cd ~
  • "$" at the beginning of commands is just the prompt. Don't include it in the command.
    $ eval "$(ssh-agent -s)"
